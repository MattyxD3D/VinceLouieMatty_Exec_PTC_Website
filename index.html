About Us

Pateros Technological College (PTC) is a community college established in 1993. It was built to offer technical-vocational courses, hence its name. The college also has a partnership with the Technological University of the Philippines (TUP), which will provide undergraduate programs to be housed in PTC.

Since its foundation, the school is committed to the development of the Filipino youth, providing them with a high-quality education. PTC has an Executive Class Program that provides educational opportunities for the out of school youth. Students of PTC are given an education that will enhance their skills and knowledge and mold them into future leaders who will contribute to the growth of their country.

Our Programs

The Executive Class runs in six (6) semesters or equivalent to two (2) school years. The classes (synchronous and asynchronous) will be held every 6PM-9PM (weekdays) and Saturdays which will commence on February 26. Applicants age 21 and above should have taken at least two (2) semesters in college or a high school graduate before the implementation of the K-12/Senior High School Program in 2015.


This is a paying trimester modular classes dedicated to WORKING INDIVIDUALS (private and government) who wish to take any of the following programs:

Bachelor Of Science in Information Technology

The emphasis of this four-year degree is on technology and computers. Its main goal is to prepare students for the rapidly changing demands of the IT sector by teaching them the fundamentals of computer hardware, software, databases, algorithms, telecommunications, user strategies, web development, application testing, and computer graphics. Bachelor Of Science in Office Administration

The Bachelor of Science in Office Administration is a four-year degree program using ladderized curriculum. Its three areas of specialization are: Corporate Transcription, Legal Transcription, and Medical Transcription from which students choose on his/her start on the third year level. The program is designed to equip its graduates the professional skills and competencies needed to meet the needs of the workplaces, whether in general business, legal, or the medical offices.
